Welcome to MJ FARMS

where freshness meets purpose ...

At MJ FARMS, we believe that healthy living starts with healthy farming. Based in Sunyani, we specialize in growing fresh, organic vegetables and supplying ethically raised poultry products to homes, restaurants, and vendors in our community. Whether you're cooking for your family, running a kitchen, or stocking a market stall, MJ FARMS brings the farm to your doorstep—clean, reliable, and full of flavor.We believe that good food starts with good soil, care, and community.MJ FARMS is your trusted source for fresh, organic vegetables and quality poultry products.

What We Offer

Organic Vegetables

Grown with care and free from harmful chemicals, our vegetables are harvested fresh from our farm to your table. From leafy greens to root crops, we ensure every bite is packed with nutrition and flavor.

Poultry Products

Our partnership with a local poultry farm allows us to offer fresh eggs and healthy poultry products, raised ethically and with high standards of hygiene.

Delivery Services

Convenience is key. We deliver farm-fresh vegetables and poultry products right to your doorstep. Just place your order, and we’ll handle the rest—quick, clean, and reliable.

Local Market Supply

We serve households, restaurants, and vendors with consistent, high-quality produce. Whether you're cooking for your family or feeding a crowd, MJ FARMS has you covered.
